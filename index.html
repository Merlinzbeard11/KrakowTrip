Getting around · car, transport & what to carry

How you move & what to pack

Short version: rent a car for Days 5–7 only (mountains + Ojców). City days are faster on foot with Bolt/Uber — and you legally cannot drive into the Old Town (pedestrianized, permit-only, camera-enforced).

🚗

The car — one 3-day block

Pick up morning of Day 5, return evening of Day 7. Days 1–4 & 8 don't need it. Park overnight (Day 6→7) at a hotel/Park & Ride or the Galeria Krakowska garage. Bring US license + IDP + passport + credit card; request an automatic (manuals are default). Compact/mid-size has enough power.

📱

In the city — Bolt, Uber & trams

Bolt & Uber both run (Bolt usually cheapest; surges in rain). Trams/buses via the jakdojade app. Airport ↔ centre: train ~20 min (~€4.50), Bolt ~25–30 min , or Bus 300 .

Plan B · deviate without derailing

Backup plans

The four day-trip days (4–7) are the flexible ones. If you'd rather not leave the city — bad weather, a slow morning, no appetite for the drive — swap in a Kraków day. If you want to go further afield instead, here are strong alternatives to the planned trips.

How to decide on the day

Hot & clear → the outdoors wins: Zakrzówek lagoon, the lake, or the mountains. Rainy or cold → go underground or indoors: a salt mine, Rynek Underground, or the Czartoryski's Leonardo. Aug 15 (holiday) → museums/shops shut and churches overflow — best as an outdoors or pilgrimage day (Częstochowa is heaving that day; beautiful but plan for crowds).

Straight swaps for the planned days

  • Day 4 (Wieliczka) → Bochnia (quieter salt mine) or a city museum day.
  • Days 5–6 (Zakopane) — don't want the 2-hr drive + overnight? → a single-day Dunajec rafting trip, or stay in the city (Zakrzówek + Czartoryski). You then also skip the extra Zakopane night (−).
  • Day 7 (Ojców) → Tyniec Abbey (much closer, half-day) or a slow city day.
  • Any rainy day → the two salt mines and Rynek Underground are weatherproof; the Czartoryski is the indoor showstopper.

Drive times and the Dunajec season/price are from current 2026 listings — reconfirm hours (several close Mondays) and book Auschwitz's free timed entry well ahead.
